## Analysis of "kore de onegai shimasu" (これでお願いします)

### Components:

- **kore (これ)**: Demonstrative pronoun meaning "this"
- **de (で)**: Particle indicating means or method
- **onegai (お願い)**: Request or "please"
- **shimasu (します)**: Polite form of the verb "to do"
